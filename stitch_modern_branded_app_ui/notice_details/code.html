All students are hereby informed that the mid-term examinations for the Spring 2024 semester are scheduled to commence from November 15, 2024.

The examinations will be conducted in two shifts: Morning (09:00 AM - 11:00 AM) and Afternoon (02:00 PM - 04:00 PM). Detailed room assignments are provided in the attached PDF document below.

Important Instructions:

  • Strict adherence to the seating plan is mandatory.
  • Students must carry their valid digital admit cards and University IDs.
  • No electronic gadgets (smartwatches, mobile phones) are allowed inside the venue.
  • Late arrival (beyond 15 minutes) will lead to disqualification from the session.

Ensure your profile is updated on the DCMS portal to avoid discrepancies in your attendance and grade reporting.
